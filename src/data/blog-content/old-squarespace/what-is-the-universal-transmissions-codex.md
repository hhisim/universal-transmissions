It is an amalgamation of many things, but to be more direct it is the result of an alchemical process; a concentrated distillation of my entire life's journey up this point (44 Years).

The Codex is a book that cannot be read, its purpose is NOT to be read. It is a book that was not written, but rather one that was woven into existence with a childlike fascination towards linguistics, etymology, semiotics, occulted esoterica, ecstatic trance states, the dream world and visionary experiences.

Weaved together with specific attention to the interrelation of sound, frequency, form, vibration and their and connection to an expansive syncretic synthesis. The Codex was not written, but captured, processed, distilled and then crafted with fanatic attention to detail coupled with an intense love of art, design, and magic.

Containing alphabets and syntax that I had created and re-remembered, It does have many parts that could potentially be "read". It also contains vast parts that are unintelligible in a linguistic sense, which play more to support the aesthetic quality and "inner meaning" of the particular page. Trying to "read" or decipher the codex, in my personal opinion, is completely missing the point and a waste of precious time.

The Universal Transmissions codex is an intentionally crafted enigma which creates a bold statement by its existence alone; Knowledge is borrowed, and Gnosis is absolute. The act of "learning" via reading, hearing, seeing is acquired and assumed until it is experienced. Gnosis is the distillation of experience. So it's not about trying to read the book, but rather experience it.

The Codex is an intentionally crafted experience that aims the initiate to lose themselves in that magic moment of wonder. The lack of linguistic value juxtaposed with an intense awareness of profound meaning, triggered by its visual semiotic construct. In other words, the experience is akin to "experiencing" a picture book, perhaps an encyclopedia, as a child who had not yet learned to read. Magic, wonder, and yet still meaning.

The roots of the Universal Transmissions Codex go back to my childhood, where I would fill journals with incomprehensible text and alphabets which I would remember from my dreams and pair them up with strange imagery. This is a practice I believe all children experience in their childhood in one form or another.

As I grew, and disconnected with inner self and identified more with the world and its struggles, those journals and the strange text, were all but forgotten. Until I re-discovered and re-remembered them in deep ecstatic states of trance. The Xenolinguistics became a bridge to connect my childhood and then present experience to begin weaving the first pages of what was going to become the Universal Transmissions Codex.

Almost exactly 10 years later, the first volume of the codex has manifested and ready for its owners.
