I am very excited and inspired as I enter into the last half of completing the Codex. This test print turned out to be of exceptional quality and is an important milestone in the evolution of this project. 

The Codex, when complete, will be 300+ pages of esoteric oddity and profound strangeness, retrieved from high-frequency, multi-dimensional data streams with subjects ranging from geometrical systems to xenodimensional life forms. From energetic vortexes to celestial mechanics. From frequency and form to transcedence and self-realization. 

The final phase begins soon after integrating the Energy Vortex (Chakra Series) pages. 

Last year, during a very tumultuous time in my life, I experienced a period of profound revelation graced with the Samadhi experience. I was revealed sets of techniques for attaining transcendence, and utilizing some of them, I was able to pull myself out of the darkest moments and period of my life into the grace and glorious simplicity of unfiltered existence, into the transcendent beauty of the eternal now. 

Later, after months of integration, I realized that what I had discovered in the dark was the Vigyan Bhairava Tantra: an ancient, tried and true 112 meditations for self realization and transcendence, an amazing, powerful, timeless dialogue between Shiva and Shakti.The heart and soul of true Tantra.

To honor this transformative experience, I decided to integrate the Vigyan Bhairava Tantra into the Codex. 

The final 200+ pages will be personal and intimate renditions of the 112 techniques and meditations. Each technique/page will have a QR Code embedded in it, which will allow a seeker to "decode" the technique into concise English. (Links to a PDF manual will also be provided for those who wish not to use "smart" technology to decode the data streams.

After 9 years and a lifetime of experience, the Codex is finally coming to fruition. Please stay tuned and be sure to check out the Patreon to support the project or integrate yourself into the experience.

Be love,

Hakan.
